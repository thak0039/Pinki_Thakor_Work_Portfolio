About Me

Hi! My Name is Pinki Thakor. I am Web Designer & Developer. I love designing top-grade UI’s with working logic to create a dynamic user experience. I am a self-motivated and self-taught professional who likes to solve problems. My primary focus is on building UX/UI design for different devices, Front End Web Applications, and Native Mobile Applications.

My skillsets include languages like JavaScript, React Native, CSS3, HTML5, C# together with frameworks like React JS, Node JS, and Bootstrap. I was fortunate enough to get a place as a UX/UI Designer & Developer to develop a Volunteers App for Experify health hospital. I have always had an interest in human behavior and interpersonal interaction and I am hounded to get the role of UX Designer at experify project that allows me to further my understanding of people and what makes them tick, what motivates them to make a decision.

I am a Mobile Application Design and Development (Honours) 2020 graduate from Algonquin College here in Canada's capital with a 3.9/4 GPA in the last semester. On a side note, I am passionate about learning, problem-solving, and trying new things. I am a hard worker and a strong communicator. I can't wait to see what challenges and opportunities lie ahead.
